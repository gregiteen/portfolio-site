A full AI workspace platform — assistants, workflows, telephony, email, and a
skill marketplace — where the tenant workspace is a virtual filesystem of SSSS
primitives. Postgres is a projection; the vault is canonical. Workspaces
export as `.ucw` bundles and re-provision anywhere with one command.
