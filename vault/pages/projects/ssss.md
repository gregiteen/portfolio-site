A vendor-neutral standard for file-native software: documents with semantic
YAML frontmatter as primitives, a deterministic Operation Contract for every
mutation, portable `.ucw` bundles, and an idempotent provisioning verb set.
Ships as `@ssss/cli` with a reference engine and a canonical conformance
suite — this very portfolio is an ssss vault.
