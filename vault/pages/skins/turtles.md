# DESIGN.md - Abyssal Architecture (The Turtle Thesis)

## 1. Visual Identity & Intent
The 'Turtles' prompt is abstractly reinterpreted away from swamp-green biology and channeled into 'Abyssal Architecture'—the mathematical, load-bearing perfection of deep-sea scute tessellations. The environment is the Mariana Trench: immense pressure, perfect geometric self-defense, and glowing fiber-optic telemetry.

## 2. Structural & Layout Philosophy
The design replaces standard rounded cards with ultra-dense, 1px bordered 'scute' grid blocks. Using CSS Subgrid and CSS-native :has() selectors, hovering over any card triggers an instant structural focusing event: the surrounding scutes dynamically dim, while the focused card glows with a glassmorphic micro-noise pattern. This structural weight conveys a biological machine engineered to survive extreme conditions.

## 3. Responsive Execution
Mobile layouts utilize a stacked vertical column flow with defensive wrap mechanisms inside headers to prevent text and button overlaps. Typography relies strictly on tabular monospace families to simulate high-precision instruments. Touch-targets are enforced globally with a 44px baseline to guarantee effortless navigation underwater or on mobile screens.
