# DESIGN.md

## Vision
An architectural fusion of Nordic wabi-sabi ceramic craft and local, file-native system engineering. The identity mirrors a quiet, monastic ceramic workshop, showcasing physical imperfection and structural code integrity.

## Palette & Texture
Built upon Oatmeal background (#F5F2EB) and slip-white (#FAF8F5) modules that invoke drying porcelain. Text elements utilize a heavy charcoal black (#1F2020) and raw iron-oxide grey (#3A3C3B) to convey physical structure and high-contrast editorial value.

## Typography
An organic serif display typeface mirrors hand-carved kiln stamps and raw physical typography. This sits in stark, beautiful contrast with an ultra-rigid monospace typeface used for systems architecture details and navigation.

## Layout & Motion
Rejecting digital grids, the structure utilizes asymmetrical shelves and uneven, organic border radii. Interactive hover states respond slowly—like clay yielding to hands—using deep, deliberate ease-out curves instead of hyperactive digital transitions.
